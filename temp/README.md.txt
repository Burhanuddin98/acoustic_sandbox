# 🔊 Acoustic Sandbox

Upload audio, apply a 20-band EQ, simulate room acoustics, or measure your own room IR using a sweep signal and microphone.

Built with Streamlit, NumPy, SciPy, Librosa, and love.

## Features

- 🎧 Upload WAV/MP3/OGG
- 🎚️ Graphic EQ (20 bands)
- 🌍 Reverb: synthetic or real (measured from your mic)
- 📊 Spectrogram plots
- ⬇️ Download processed output
